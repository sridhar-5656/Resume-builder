Resumes by Canva

Making a resume that stands out from the slush pile is easier and more convenient with Canva. You can follow a pre-made resume template or create your own design from scratch using our intuitive drag-and-drop editor tool. All resume templates measure 21 × 29.7 cm or 8 × 11 inches (A4 size), but you can resize them to whatever document format you prefer. Easily order resumes as printed letterheads through Canva Print and choose between standard (120 gsm) and premium (148 gsm) paper stock types.
